The labryinth of Suffering

Suffering, might sound a heavy word to us adoloscents.
But we never know that the one of the main cause of our suffering in later age might start in our tender age as well.
In fact, most of the teenagers of present generation are suffering from depression, secretly. And the most shocking part is they have mastered the art of concealing their thoughts, their inner feelings, soo perfectly that if they ever, ever break down and burst up into tears, letting out all their bottled up emotions, the senior , mature and "understanding" department of the house would then start questioning their own life,their own brain and their own bond between the children. It was never a bond for few, justa formality in which they only did the hearing of scorns The youngsters are given soo much exposure, that it leads them to overthink soo deeply and leads them into despair. This does not mean that a parent should prevent a child from getting such an exposure,because that would lead to the feeling of being excluded and left over among the friends. Who says that the children know nothing about the society? They do know, dont underestimate them, please. They know much about society, at least of theirs, if not of the society, the parents always refer to, and before anyone asks, yes there is a society among youngsters.
